EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
----------------------------------------------------------------

TechCorp Industries delivered a strong third quarter in 2023, with total revenue of $12.4 million representing a 14% year-over-year increase from Q3 2022 ($10.9M). Net operating income for the quarter was $2.1 million (16.9% margin), consistent with our full-year profitability targets. Headcount at quarter close stood at 312 full-time employees, an increase of 18 from the Q2 close of 294.

The quarter saw above-plan performance in our Enterprise Solutions division, offset by moderate underperformance in the SMB segment, which continues to face longer sales cycles attributed to macroeconomic caution among smaller buyers.

Q4 2023 FORECAST
----------------------------------------------------------------

Based on current pipeline visibility and signed commitments, TechCorp projects Q4 2023 revenue of $13.8M – $14.4M, representing 11–16% YoY growth. Full-year 2023 revenue guidance is maintained at $46.5M – $47.5M.

Key assumptions: continued Enterprise momentum, at least 5 new logo closings before December 31, and no material customer churn. The sales pipeline entering Q4 stands at $34.2M in qualified opportunities, of which $18.6M is in late-stage (Stage 4+).

Project Atlas, the legacy data migration initiative, is expected to complete in Q4 2023, which will reduce infrastructure costs by an estimated $85,000 per quarter beginning Q1 2024.
