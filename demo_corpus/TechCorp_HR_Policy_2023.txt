SECTION 1 — SCOPE AND PURPOSE
----------------------------------------------------------------

This policy manual applies to all full-time and part-time employees of TechCorp Industries and its wholly owned subsidiaries. It supersedes all prior HR policy documents dated before January 1, 2023. Employees are expected to review this document upon hire and at each annual revision.

Questions regarding any policy in this manual should be directed to the People Operations team at hr@techcorp.io or by submitting a ticket through the internal HR portal (HRConnect).

----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2 — REMOTE WORK POLICY
----------------------------------------------------------------

TechCorp Industries supports a hybrid work model that balances team collaboration with individual flexibility.

Eligible employees may work remotely for up to THREE (3) days per week. At minimum, employees must be present in their assigned office location two (2) days per week to attend scheduled team meetings, one-on-ones, and any department-wide sessions.

Remote workdays must be pre-approved by the employee's direct manager and logged in the scheduling tool (Workday) no later than 48 hours in advance. Approval is at managerial discretion based on team needs and project deadlines.

Employees working remotely are expected to maintain the same availability, response times, and professional standards as they would in the office. Core collaboration hours are 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM in the employee's primary time zone.

Employees in roles designated as "on-site required" (including facilities, lab operations, and certain customer support tiers) are not eligible for remote work arrangements.

----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3 — PAID TIME OFF (PTO)
----------------------------------------------------------------

Full-time employees accrue twenty (20) days of paid time off per calendar year, available for vacation, personal leave, or non-emergency illness. PTO begins accruing from the first day of employment at a rate of 1.67 days per month.

Up to five (5) unused PTO days may be rolled over into the following calendar year. Any PTO balance exceeding the rollover cap is forfeited on December 31.

PTO requests must be submitted a minimum of five (5) business days in advance for absences of three or more consecutive days. Requests of one or two days may be submitted with shorter notice, subject to manager approval.

----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 4 — PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
----------------------------------------------------------------

TechCorp conducts formal performance reviews on an annual basis. Review periods run January through December, with evaluations completed in January of the following year.

The review process consists of a self-assessment submitted by the employee, a manager evaluation, and a calibration session at the department level facilitated by People Operations. Final ratings are communicated to employees by February 15 each year.

Merit increases and equity adjustments, where applicable, are effective March 1 following the review cycle.

----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 5 — ONBOARDING
----------------------------------------------------------------

New employees complete a structured 30-day onboarding program that includes system access setup, compliance training (security awareness, data handling, anti-harassment), role-specific technical orientation, and an introduction to TechCorp's culture and values.

Each new hire is assigned an onboarding buddy from their immediate team for the first 60 days.

----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 6 — EQUIPMENT AND HOME OFFICE ALLOWANCE
----------------------------------------------------------------

Employees approved for hybrid or remote work arrangements are eligible for a one-time equipment allowance of $800 to support a functional home office setup. Eligible purchases include monitors, keyboards, webcams, ergonomic accessories, and networking equipment.

Receipts must be submitted through Concur within 90 days of start date or policy approval date. Allowance does not roll over and is non-transferable.

TechCorp will provide a company laptop and required software licenses to all employees regardless of work location.

----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 7 — TRAVEL AND EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
----------------------------------------------------------------

Business travel must be pre-approved by the employee's department head. All flights should be booked through the approved corporate travel portal (Egencia). Economy class is required for domestic flights; business class is permitted for international flights exceeding six hours.

Hotel bookings are capped at $250 per night in Tier 1 markets (San Francisco, New York, Boston) and $175 per night in all other markets.

Meal reimbursement is up to $75 per day with itemized receipts. Alcohol is not reimbursable.

Expense reports must be submitted within 30 days of trip completion.

----------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 8 — CODE OF CONDUCT
----------------------------------------------------------------

TechCorp expects all employees to act with integrity, respect, and professionalism in all interactions — with colleagues, clients, vendors, and the public. Discrimination, harassment, and retaliation of any kind are strictly prohibited and subject to disciplinary action up to and including termination.

Employees must report any suspected policy violations to People Operations or through the anonymous Ethics Hotline (1-800-555-0192).
